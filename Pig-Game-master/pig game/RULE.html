Game Rules

1. You have to set your winning score(and press 'OK') which will be updated per match.(default value is always 100)

2. After each roll if any of the dice obtain '1' no score will be added to the total score and immediately will switch to other player.

3. You will be able to roll your dice infinitely until any of the dice scores '1' or you choose 'HOLD'.

4. By pressing 'HOLD' button you will be able to add your current score with the total score and the player will be switched immediately.

5. After each match pressing 'New Game' will allow you to start another round.(Don't forget to set your winning score)
